Move Reminder Free allows you to use your phone as a pedometer and receive reminder notifications if you've been idle for too long.

Do I need to keep the phone with me?

Yes, you will need to keep your phone with you to detect step count.

Does this app need to stay to open?

No, your steps will still be counted and you will receive reminders even when the app is closed.

Will this app work if my device does not have a step count sensor?

It will vary by device. Try checking the step count after using the app for a few hours and decide if the accuracy is good enough for your use.
